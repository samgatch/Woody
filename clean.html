Common Repairs/ Cleaning

Cleaning

All gas fireplaces require cleaning once a year. This prevents buildup of soot and other debris from outside, it also helps the unit run more efficiently. This can also help track future issues and may prevent needing a repair in the future.

Repairs

Whether the flame is going out, you smell gas,broken logs, or the unit does nothing at all we can fix it. When working with anything electronic it is inevitable that it will eventually need a repair. We confidently say we can fix any problem a fireplace can have and we will do it professionally and in a timely manner!
